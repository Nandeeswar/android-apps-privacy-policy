**Privacy Policy for Wave Control**

Effective Date: 13-03-2026
Thank you for using Wave Control. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.
1. Camera Usage (Local Processing Only)
Wave Control requires access to your device's camera to detect hand gestures.
•
No Recording: The app does not record, take photos, or save videos.
•
On-Device Processing: All camera frames are processed in real-time locally on your device.
•
No Transmission: No camera data is ever sent to our servers or any third parties.
2. Phone and Call Management
The app requests permissions to read phone state and manage calls. This is used solely to allow you to accept or reject incoming calls using hand gestures. We do not access your call logs or contact lists.
3. Accessibility Services
Wave Control uses the AccessibilityServices API to perform horizontal screen swipes. This is required only for the "Air-Wave Gallery" feature to navigate photos hands-free. We do not collect or monitor any data through this service.
4. Notification Access
The app requires Notification Access to interact with your music players (like Spotify or YouTube). This is used only to send "Play/Pause" and "Next/Previous" commands.
5. Data Collection and Sharing
•
No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
•
Offline Functionality: The app is designed to work entirely offline.
•
No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
